# PRODUCT.md — HeiPard

## What this is

Direct-to-consumer e-commerce site (Shopify) for HeiPard, a German brand for decorative and functional lighting: string lights, fairy lights, festoon lights, solar garden lights, camping lights, and a small smart lighting line. The site sells *Stimmung* (ambiance and atmosphere), not electronics. Brand of C&L Handels GmbH.

## Audience

Two equally important groups: (1) camping and outdoor enthusiasts who light tents, campers, and gardens, and (2) home & decor lovers who create cozy interiors and balconies. Mostly German consumers, mobile-heavy traffic, gifting and seasonal peaks (Christmas dominates H2).

## Brand personality & voice

Warm, cozy, atmospheric, premium-but-approachable. Informal German "du" throughout. Tagline: "Lasse deine Welt erleuchten". Confident and warm, never salesy, never technical-cold. Short, sensory sentences ("Licht, das den Abend verlängert."). No discount screaming, no exclamation-mark marketing.

## Visual system

- Light mode "Tag" (default): warm cream `#FAF6F0`, warm charcoal text `#2B2926`, terracotta `#C96F4A` primary, pine green `#2D4A3E` secondary, logo orange `#E87A2E`
- Dark mode "Abendstimmung": warm night blue `#1B2433` — evening sky, never pure black, never tech-dark
- Signature feature: a physical-style light switch in the header toggling the modes with a soft glow transition
- Typography: characterful warm serif for headlines, friendly modern sans for UI/body, terracotta uppercase kicker labels above section headlines
- Imagery: photographic only, warm light sources visible in frame, dusk/evening mood, serving both outdoor and home scenes
- Motion: subtle glow on cards and CTAs, gentle scroll reveals — refined, never carnival

## References

Primary: the final Lovable mockup screenshots in `docs/mockups/`. External: Twinkly (premium decorative lighting presentation), Nanoleaf (light-mode clarity). Govee only as a dark-mode professionalism benchmark — do not copy its tech coldness.

## Anti-patterns (hard constraints)

- Amazon-A+-style infographic images as product page content; text baked into images
- Cheap marketplace aesthetics: discount badges, crossed-out prices, cluttered seals
- Cold tech/gadget styling; teal/turquoise accents; purple/blue gradients; glassmorphism; gradient text
- Grayscale image treatments; illustrations mixed into photography; clinical cold-white scenes
- Origin claims of any kind ("Made in", "Designed in Germany", "deutsche Ingenieurskunst") — legally risky; trust is communicated only through factual signals (shipping from Germany, German support, product-specific warranty)
- Invented facts: founding years, warranty extras, shipping conditions — mark unknowns as [TBD] instead

## Sister brand boundary

LUMOnova (same company) owns the calm, editorial-dark, smart-tech-premium space. HeiPard must stay brighter, more natural, more emotional — the two brands must never look interchangeable.
